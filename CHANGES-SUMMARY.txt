🚀 GETTING STARTED (3 STEPS)

1. UPDATE config.js
   window.SUPABASE_URL = 'https://wausefmzaqtlomyhqcjf.supabase.co';
   window.SUPABASE_ANON = 'your-anon-key-here';

2. DISABLE EMAIL CONFIRMATIONS IN SUPABASE
   Supabase Dashboard → Authentication → Settings → Email Auth
   → Uncheck "Enable email confirmations" → Save

3. TEST IT
   - Click Sign Up, fill form, create account
   - Click Sign In, enter credentials, get redirected
